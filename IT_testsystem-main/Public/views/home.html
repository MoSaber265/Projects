Our Identity

Vision & Mission

We believe that quality technical education is the gateway to comprehensive digital transformation

Our Vision

To be a leading college in the region in the fields of computing and information systems, contributing to building an advanced knowledge society that keeps pace with technological transformations and leads digital entrepreneurship locally and regionally.

Our Mission

To provide integrated academic and applied education in computer and information disciplines, create a stimulating research environment for creativity and innovation, and qualify graduates capable of competing and serving the requirements of digital development.
